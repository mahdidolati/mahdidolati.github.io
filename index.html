Short Bio

I’ve got my Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Engineering from University of Tehran and Masters degree from Sharif University of Technology. I’ve done my PhD under supervision of Professor Ahmad Khonsari in Computer Engineering at University of Tehran. I was a postdoctoral researcher at IPM. I'm currently an Assistant Professor in the Computer Engineering Department at Sharif University of Technology.
